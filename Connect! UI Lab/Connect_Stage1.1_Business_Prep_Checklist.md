# Connect! — Stage 1.1 Business Prep Checklist

*Drafted 2026-06-01 from project memory (the master checklist file wasn't reachable this session, so verify against it when you can). Stage 1.1 was intentionally deferred so you could push into 1.2 Design — this is the catch-up list. Kept lean per the solo-builder simplicity bias; items you can safely defer are flagged.*

Status legend: ✅ done · 🔄 in progress · ⬜ to do · ⏭️ defer (with note)

---

## 1. Brand & identity

- ⬜ Lock the app name. "Connect!" is the working name — confirm it's final, or decide a launch name.
- ⬜ Check name availability: Qatar trade-name register, Apple App Store / Google Play, `.qa` and `.com` domains, and Instagram/social handles (same handle across all).
- ⬜ Logo + wordmark (even a simple AI-assisted v1 is fine for Stage 1).
- ⬜ Define core brand basics: primary color, one-line tagline, the 1-sentence pitch ("the app that connects padel/tennis/badminton players in Doha").
- ⏭️ Full brand guidelines / multiple logo variations — defer; a single clean logo + color is enough to launch.

## 2. Domain & email

- ⬜ Register a domain (`.com` and/or `.qa`). A landing/waitlist page can live here pre-launch.
- ⬜ Set up a founder/business email on the domain (e.g. hello@ or jianming@). Use it for App Store, socials, and partner outreach so it's all in one identity.

## 3. Social presence  *(ties into your marketing plan)*

- 🔄 **Instagram account** — already in your plan: 3 posts + 1–2 reels/week, batched Saturdays, founder-led, AI-assisted content. Create the handle + bio + link if not done.
- ⬜ Reserve matching handles on any platform you might use later (even if dormant) so names stay consistent.
- 🔄 **WhatsApp group infiltration** — get into 5–10 Doha padel/tennis/badminton/running groups; participate, become a known name. (Pre-launch traction work.)
- ⏭️ TikTok, Facebook groups, Reddit, etc. — explicitly not chosen; skip unless you reopen the channel list.

## 4. Legal & policy  *(mostly already done)*

- ✅ Terms of Service — drafted (EN + AR).
- ✅ Privacy Policy — drafted (EN + AR).
- ✅ Community Standards — drafted (EN + AR).
- ✅ Confirm 18+ positioning is consistent across all three docs (Terms §2, Privacy §11, Community Standards — EN+AR, done 2026-06-01). Store-listing age rating ⏭️ deferred to Stage 2 — set when the app actually goes live on the Apple App Store / Google Play (Stage 1 is web-first PWA, no store listing yet).
- ⏭️ Final lawyer review of all legal docs — batched for one pass at the END of Stage 1 (pre-launch), per your plan. Don't do it piecemeal now.
- ⬜ Note for later: Qatar data-protection (PDPPL) basics — flag for the pre-launch legal pass.

## 5. Business registration & money

- ✅ Legal entity: launch Stage 1 as a **personal founder project** (decided 2026-06-01). No company registration this stage.
- ⏭️ Business bank account / payment processor — **deferred to the native-app launch phase** (when you move everything to a real app and go live on the App Store / Google Play). Stage 1 is free + cash-on-arrival, no in-app payment, so no merchant account needed yet.
- ⏭️ Developer accounts — Apple Developer ($99/yr) and Google Play ($25 one-time) — **deferred to the native-app launch phase.** Stage 1 is a **web app (PWA) for testing**, so no store accounts are required yet.

## 6. Tooling & accounts  *(needed for the build phase)*

- ✅ Tech stack chosen — Cursor + Claude Code + Supabase + GitHub (web-first PWA).
- ⬜ Create the project accounts under the business email: GitHub, Supabase, plus a domain registrar and Instagram. One identity, one password manager.
- ⬜ Set up a basic analytics/feedback path so you can measure toward the 500-active-players goal once live (can be lightweight in Stage 1).

## 7. Goals & positioning  *(quick to write down, keeps you aligned)*

- ⬜ Write the one-paragraph positioning: who it's for (Doha padel/tennis/badminton players, 18+), the problem (hard to find people to play with), the promise (open + approval-based matches, free).
- ⬜ Restate the Stage 1 success metric: 500 active players by ~Month 10, launch ~Month 4.

---

## Suggested order (highest leverage first)

1. **Name + domain + handles check** (do these together — you want one consistent identity before you register anything).
2. **Register domain + set up founder email.**
3. **Create accounts under that email** (GitHub/Supabase/IG).
4. **Simple logo + tagline + positioning paragraph.**
5. Everything else (entity decision, dev accounts, analytics) can trail behind without blocking the build.

## Deferred out of 1.1 (don't let these slow you down)

Lawyer review (→ end of Stage 1); developer accounts, business bank/payment, and the store-listing age rating (→ all deferred to the native-app launch phase, when you go live on Apple App Store / Google Play); full brand guidelines; company registration (Stage 1 launches as a personal founder project); and any marketing channel you didn't pick.
