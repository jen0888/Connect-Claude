# Connect! — Doha Venues List (Stage 1 seed)

*Compiled 2026-05-30 from public sources (Time Out Doha, Visit Qatar, Playo, Playtomic, Property Finder, Education City, Aspire Zone, Al Bidda Park, ILoveQatar, BadmintonBlaze, Doha Oasis, Dana Club). Confirm exact court counts and addresses before final seeding — these can change. Scope narrowed 2026-06-01 to padel, tennis and badminton only (squash and football removed).*

This is the curated list for the **Choose Location** picker when a player creates a match. Players pick from here; a **custom venue** entry is always available as a fallback (per the venue-picker design). Court # is a separate field, entered by the host after picking a venue.

## How to read the Sports column

P = Padel · T = Tennis · Bd = Badminton

*Indoor venues matter most May–September (heat). Indoor padel is plentiful; indoor tennis is rare — Al Dana Club, West Bay Tennis Club, Sheraton Grand Doha, and the Aspire Dome.*

## Notes before seeding

- Entries marked *(confirm)* have a best-guess district — verify the exact area/address before launch.
- **Indoor padel:** rows 1–6. **Indoor tennis:** rows 13, 14, 15 and the Aspire Dome (row 22). All other tennis venues are outdoor — important to surface in the picker for summer play.
- "Padel In" runs several branches; I've listed the main Doha-area ones. Sealine is outside the city, so it's omitted for a Doha-only Stage 1.
- Court counts intentionally left out of the picker (hosts pick venue + enter their own Court #).
- **Scope (2026-06-01):** padel, tennis, badminton only. Squash and football venues removed. Khalifa keeps its official name (Tennis & Squash Complex) but is tagged tennis-only. Aspire Dome and Dynamic Sports Academy are multi-sport venues kept for their tennis / badminton courts respectively.
- This same venue table is reusable for Stage 3 (court booking), so keep the schema stable.
