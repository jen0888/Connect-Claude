## Notes

- "Full" (ممتلئة) and "Completed" (منتهية) are deliberately different words — don't merge them.
- "Venue/Location" uses المكان in the picker; the physical court itself is الملعب (used in "Court number" = رقم الملعب).
- Confirm these read naturally with a native Doha user during the review round; adjust as a set, not per-screen.
